About Us.

We sell shirts and hoodies to support local STEM programs across multiple DISD schools!

Our organization strives to help educate all people equally about the importance of the STEM field. By creating funds to teach this field we hope to break boundaries and empower minorities in the STEM field to learn more and hopefully follow a career in a Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math field. All proceeds from sales go back into DISD schools and educational STEM kits that can do more than create funds: it can create inspiration. Please buy a shirt or hoodie to support children across Dallas.
